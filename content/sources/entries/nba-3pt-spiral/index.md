An interactive Three.js spiral that charts the NBA's three-point revolution — from the ABA experiment in the 1960s to today's corner-three dominated game.

Each point on the spiral represents a season. Hover to see three-point attempt rate, make rate, and context for major inflection points (the Steph Curry era, the Houston Rockets analytics experiment, etc.).
